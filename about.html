Hamari Kahani

From the Royal Kitchens of Rajasthan

The Royal Restaurant was born from a dream — Chef Ramesh Sharma's dream of bringing the authentic flavours of Rajasthan's royal kitchens to the heart of New Delhi. In 1998, with a handful of ancestral recipes and an unwavering passion for pure vegetarian cooking, he opened the doors of what would become the city's most beloved Indian restaurant.

Every dish on our menu tells a story. The Dal Makhani recipe has been slow-cooked the same way for three generations. The Rajasthani Thali is a tribute to the grand feasts of the Maharajas. Our Gulab Jamun is made fresh every morning, just as Chef Ramesh's mother taught him.

Today, The Royal Restaurant is more than a restaurant — it is a home away from home for anyone who loves authentic Indian food, warm hospitality, and the soul of India on a plate.
